Season System

Versatility

The season system aims to balance the game with each new season and give our team the chance to analyze what is unbalanced, what is difficult, what is easy and adjust each season, so we hope that with each new season we will have a fairer, more balanced game with new changes and new items.

How will the items work?

With the arrival of a new season, some items will no longer be available in the game, this will make it unique and exclusive! Within each season we will give you the opportunity to obtain rare and exclusive items both by purchasing in the store and in game, and this is our idea so that you can make the game's economy wheel turn on its own. Got an exclusive item? You can sell it for gold, Ethereal points or Ethercoin, you decide how you want to earn, the season system was made for this, making rare and exclusive items even more exclusive! Take a look at Ethercoin in this thread.

Restarting the Game

Even though the title of this topic is scary, calm down, breathe... Each season we will reset kill points, death points, battle points, guild rating points and reset stones, we will also open an exclusive tab here in our website for you to take a peek at what's coming next season and for those who wish to reset their skill points and status, send an email during this period to our support team. The best guilds will receive exclusive loot.
